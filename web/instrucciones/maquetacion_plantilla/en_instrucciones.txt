Layout template
=========================
1. The first step is to generate and download the structure in html
   with blocks needed for the layout.

2. After downloading the html file, you can modify to your liking both styles
    as the arrangement of the elements. It is very important to preserve the IDs of the elements of each slot

3. Once the modification of the layout must upload this file.
    During the climb, it will check that all required elements are present.
    Otherwise the file upload will be aborted and will not be stored in the system.

4. If the upload was successful, you can preview a sample of the template by clicking on the Preview button.
